1Overview概述

Nuna-Edu-Toolkit is a self-hostable, source-available platform for collecting and annotating first-person ambient audio. Sound captured by the Nuna wearable (a commercial smart necklace) is streamed to a server you control, sliced into 1-minute segments, optionally pre-labeled with ASR for fast recall, and annotated through a web app using a flexible, event-driven schema. Participants stay in control: they choose which recordings to share and can delete the rest.

The toolkit has two parts: a mobile collection app that streams audio from the wearable to your server, and Nuna Audio Lab — the web app where you review and annotate it ("Lab" = labeling). End to end: Nuna wearable → phone → your server → label & export. (You'll see the "Nuna Audio Lab" name in the app and the live demo.)

The method this toolkit is built around

This documentation describes one way to use the toolkit — and the one it is designed around: a self-recall annotation workflow for building first-person (ego) audio datasets. The person who wore the device reviews their own day (helped by timestamps and ASR previews) and labels scene, event, and emotion from memory. Because the annotations come from the person who actually lived the moment, the emotion and event ground-truth is genuinely first-person and self-reported — not a third party's guess. The label schema is web-based and easy to swap, so the same platform can serve other annotation tasks too.

Nuna-Edu-Toolkit 是一个可自部署、源码可得（source-available）的平台，用于采集与标注第一人称环境音频。由 Nuna 智能项链（一款成熟的商用可穿戴设备）采集的声音会被推流到你自己掌控的服务器，切分为 1 分钟的片段，可选地用 ASR 预标注以便快速回忆，并通过一个 Web App、以灵活的事件驱动模式进行标注。参与者始终掌握主动权：自行选择愿意共享哪些录音，其余可删除。

工具链由两部分组成：手机采集 App（将可穿戴设备的音频推流到你的服务器），以及 Nuna Audio Lab——用于回看与标注的网页应用（其中“Lab”取 labeling／标注 之意）。整条链路为：Nuna 项链 → 手机 → 你的服务器 → 标注与导出。（你会在应用与在线 Demo 中看到 “Nuna Audio Lab” 这一名称。）

本工具围绕的核心方法

本文档描述的是该工具的一种用法，也是它最初为之设计的用法：一套用于构建第一人称（ego）音频数据集的用户自回忆标注流程。佩戴设备的人借助时间戳与 ASR 预览回顾自己的一天，凭记忆标注场景、事件与情绪。由于标注来自亲历者本人，情绪与事件的 ground-truth 是真正第一人称、自我报告的，而非第三方的猜测。标注模式基于网页、易于替换，因此同一平台也可服务于其他标注任务。

2How a dataset is built数据集如何构建

Capture → slice → ASR recall → annotate → dataset. 采集 → 切片 → ASR 回忆 → 标注 → 数据集。

The wearable streams ambient sound to your ingest server, which stores the raw audio and slices it into 1-minute segments grouped by day (hour → quarter → minute). Each segment gets an optional speech-to-text preview so the wearer can recall context at a glance. Annotation is event-driven and multi-granularity: scene, event, and emotion naturally occur at very different time scales, so the schema is layered into blocks (coarse) and points (fine, sparse).

Block label — coarse

A stable stretch of time

Tens of minutes to hours, during which the environment barely changes, an ongoing activity continues, and the overall mood is stable.

  • Scene — [adjective] [noun], [note]; describes the surroundings (non-ego), ideally inferable from sound. e.g. "quiet classroom — a group sitting an exam."
  • Behavior — [adverb] [verb], [note]; the wearer's ongoing activity (ego-HAR). e.g. "attentively invigilating — pacing, reminding students."
  • Emotion — overall tone on a Valence–Arousal grid.
  • + VAD over the block (speech present / absent).
Point label — fine & sparse

A salient moment inside a block

A few minutes within a block: same scene, but a distinctive sub-event happens and shifts the mood.

  • Point event — either an environmental fluctuation [subject] [predicate], [note] (non-ego, e.g. "a glass falls and shatters") or a personal action [adverb] [verb], [note] (ego-HAR, e.g. "nervously answering a question").
  • Emotion — the mood fluctuation the point event causes (Valence–Arousal).
  • + VAD over the point.

The two-dimensional Valence–Arousal emotion model spans pleasant↔unpleasant (valence) and calm↔activated (arousal) — e.g. low-valence/high-arousal reads as tense, high-valence/low-arousal as relaxed. Blocks must cover contiguous whole segments; points cover one or more segments within their block. This is exactly the shape of the data the platform stores.

可穿戴设备将环境音推流到你的接收服务器，服务器保存原始音频并按天切分为 1 分钟片段（小时 → 刻钟 → 分钟分组）。每个片段可附带语音转文字预览，便于佩戴者一眼回忆上下文。标注以事件驱动且多粒度：场景、事件、情绪天然处于差异很大的时间尺度，因此模式被分层为块标注（粗）与点标注（细而稀疏）。

块标注 — 粗粒度

一段稳定的时间

数十分钟到数小时，期间环境几乎不变、进行着某项持续性活动、整体情绪基调稳定。

  • 场景 — 【形容词】的【名词】，【备注】；描述周围环境（非 ego），最好能由声音推断。例：「安静的教室——一群学生正在考试。」
  • 块状事件（行为） — 【副词】地【动词/动宾】，【备注】；佩戴者本人的持续性活动（ego-HAR）。例：「认真地监考——不时走动、提醒同学。」
  • 情绪 — 整段时间在 Valence–Arousal（效价–唤醒）网格上的总体基调。
  • + VAD：该块的语音活动检测（有无人声）。
点标注 — 细而稀疏

块内的一个显著时刻

块内的几分钟：场景相同，但发生了具有明显特征的子事件，并引起情绪波动。

  • 点状事件 — 二选一：环境波动 【主】【谓】，【备注】（非 ego，例：「玻璃杯掉到地上摔碎」）或 个人行动 【副词】地【动词】，【备注】（ego-HAR，例：「紧张地回答问题」）。
  • 情绪 — 该点状事件带来的情绪波动（Valence–Arousal）。
  • + VAD：该点的语音活动检测。

二维效价–唤醒（Valence–Arousal）情绪模型刻画「愉快↔不愉快」（效价）与「松弛↔激活」（唤醒）——例如低效价/高唤醒近似「紧绷」，高效价/低唤醒近似「轻松」。块必须覆盖连续的整段片段；点在所属块内覆盖一个或多个片段。这正是平台所存储数据的结构。

3Collecting & annotating (for participants)采集与标注（面向参与者）

What a data contributor does, end to end. You'll need a Nuna necklace, an Android phone, and two URLs from your study's developer: the annotation-site URL and the data-upload URL. 数据贡献者的完整流程。你需要：一条 Nuna 项链、一部安卓手机，以及由研究方（开发者）提供的两个地址：标注网站 URL 与数据上传 URL。

1Install the data-collection app安装数据采集 App

The public app-store build of the Nuna app does not upload or save audio. Install the internal-beta data-collection build instead: pgyer.com/nuna-android-cas (Android only).

应用市场的 Nuna 发行版没有音频上传/保存功能，请安装专用于数据采集的内测版：pgyer.com/nuna-android-cas（仅支持安卓）。

2Pair the necklace & start capturing连接项链并开始采集

Register an account by email, then add your device under Me → My Device → ＋ and follow the prompts. When the status shows a green Connected, the necklace is streaming ambient sound to the server. Keep the phone online — uploads are streamed, so audio captured offline may be lost.

用邮箱注册账号，在 Me → My Device → 右上角 ＋ 添加设备并按提示完成搜索配对。当状态显示绿色 Connected 时，项链已在向服务器推流采集。请保持手机联网——上传是推流机制，断网期间的数据可能丢失。

3Point uploads at your study's server将上传指向研究方服务器

So that audio goes to the developer's server (not Nuna's), set the upload server URL in the app to the data-upload URL your developer gave you, and save.

为了让音频上传到开发者服务器（而非 Nuna 官方服务器），在 App 中将上传服务器地址手动配置为开发者提供的数据上传 URL并保存。

4Review your recordings on the website在网站上查看你的录音

Log in to the annotation-site URL with the ID assigned to your Nuna account. (You may need to capture some data first before registration succeeds.) Your recordings appear as 1-minute segments with timestamps, inline playback, and ASR previews. Choose what to share; delete anything you'd rather not include.

用注册 Nuna 账号时分配的 ID，登录开发者提供的标注网站 URL。（可能需要先采集一段数据、后台有数据后才能成功注册。）你的录音以 1 分钟片段展示，带时间戳、回放与 ASR 预览。自行选择共享内容；不愿包含的可删除。

5Annotate by event: block, then point按事件标注：先块、后点

Select all segments belonging to one event and click Create Block to record scene / behavior / emotion. Annotated segments show a blue tag; open one to add finer point labels for salient sub-events and mood fluctuations. The labeled audio is saved to the developer's database, ready to become a first-person dataset.

批量选中属于同一事件的全部片段，点击 Create Block 填写场景 / 行为 / 情绪完成块标注。已标注片段会显示蓝色标签；点击进入可继续为显著子事件与情绪波动添加更细的点标注。带标签的音频会保存到开发者数据库，可用于后续构建第一人称数据集。

Try it first先试一试

The live browser demo reproduces the annotation workspace with sample data — no install, nothing saved — so you can see these screens before collecting anything.在线浏览器 Demo 用示例数据复现了标注工作台——无需安装、不保存任何数据——可在正式采集前先熟悉这些界面。

4Self-hosting (for developers)自部署（面向开发者）

Run the platform on your own server to receive participants' audio and serve the annotation UI. 在你自己的服务器上运行平台，用于接收参与者音频并提供标注界面。

You will need: Nuna necklaces to hand out to participants; a Linux server with publicly reachable data and web ports (a lightweight server is plenty); and an ASR API (optional — a no-credentials mock provider is bundled).

Data flow: necklace → phone app (push/stream) → your server.

Deploy

  1. Clone the release repo: github.com/Kevin-Pal/nuna-edu-toolkit-release.
  2. Run ./scripts/init-runtime.sh to create runtime directories and default env files.
  3. Configure the two env layers: compose/.env (host-side, e.g. ports) and runtime/env/*.env (in-container, e.g. SESSION_SECRET, CUSTOMER_SERVER_URL, ASR provider).
  4. Bring up all services with ./scripts/deploy.sh -d (app + receiver + prelabel-service).
  5. Export a snapshot of collected data with ./scripts/snapshot_and_reset.sh.

What's stored: audio/ holds the raw uploaded files; the SQLite db holds segment metadata and annotation results. Hand the participant guide above to your contributors for large-scale collection. See the repository README for the full command reference and configuration table.

你需要：发放给参与者的 Nuna 项链；一台具有公网可达的数据端口与 Web 端口的 Linux 服务器（轻量级即可）；以及一个 ASR API（可选——内置了无需凭据的 mock 提供方）。

数据流：项链 → 手机 App（推流）→ 你的服务器。

部署

  1. 克隆发布仓库：github.com/Kevin-Pal/nuna-edu-toolkit-release。
  2. 运行 ./scripts/init-runtime.sh 生成运行时目录与默认环境文件。
  3. 配置两层环境：compose/.env（宿主机侧，如端口）与 runtime/env/*.env（容器内，如 SESSION_SECRET、CUSTOMER_SERVER_URL、ASR 提供方）。
  4. 用 ./scripts/deploy.sh -d 一键启动全部服务（app + receiver + prelabel-service）。
  5. 用 ./scripts/snapshot_and_reset.sh 导出已采集数据的快照。

存储内容：audio/ 保存用户上传的原始音频；SQLite db 保存片段元信息与标注结果。将上文的参与者指南下发给贡献者以进行大规模采集。完整命令与配置项见仓库 README。

5What the dataset supports数据集支持的任务

An ego-audio dataset built this way is real-world, longitudinal, first-person, and pure-audio — with emotion and event labels reported by the wearer. Here are the established, community-relevant tasks it can serve. 以这种方式构建的 ego-audio 数据集是真实世界、纵向、第一人称、纯音频的，且情绪与事件标签由佩戴者本人报告。下面是它能服务的、社区关注的成熟任务。

Because annotation is recall-based, the dataset favours coarse-grained labels (a few labels over a long stretch) and sparse fine-grained labels (a handful of marked moments) — which fits these tasks well:

TaskFamilySupportWhat in the schema provides it
Acoustic Scene Classification (ASC)EnvironmentDirectThe block scene label (adjective + noun) over contiguous segments.
Sound Event Detection (SED)EnvironmentDirectPoint environmental-fluctuation events marked within a scene (state → fluctuation).
Voice Activity Detection (VAD)SpeechDirectPer-block / per-point VAD flags (speech present vs absent).
Human Activity Recognition (HAR)Human-centeredDirectBlock & point behavior labels = ego-HAR, both continuous and transient actions.
Affective Computing (AC)Human-centeredDirectBlock & point emotion on a Valence–Arousal grid: stable tone + fluctuations, self-reported.
First-Person PerceptionHuman-centeredDirectA pure-audio egocentric stream with Event-Ego / Emotion-Ego labels (most prior work is vision-led).
Speech Emotion Recognition (SER)SpeechCurated subsetEmotion-salient utterances pre-labeled, then user-confirmed; everyday neutral speech stays sparse.
Audio Captioning (audio↔text)ReasoningCurated subsetTemplated scene–event–emotion plus free-text notes → LLM-expanded captions, user-verified.

Direct the schema yields labels for this task as-is.   Curated subset supported for a salient, pre-screened subset rather than dense coverage.

由于标注基于回忆，数据集更适合粗粒度标签（一长段时间用少量标注）与稀疏的细粒度标签（只标注少数关键时刻）——这恰好契合下列任务：

任务类别支持度由标注模式中的什么提供
环境音分类（ASC）环境感知直接块标注的场景标签（形容词 + 名词），覆盖连续片段。
声音事件检测（SED）环境感知直接场景内标注的环境波动点状事件（环境状态 → 环境波动）。
声音活动识别（VAD）语音直接每个块/点的 VAD 标记（有无人声）。
人类活动识别（HAR）以人为中心直接块与点的行为标签 = ego-HAR，涵盖持续与短暂动作。
情感计算（AC）以人为中心直接块与点的情绪，基于效价–唤醒网格：稳定基调 + 波动，自我报告。
第一人称感知以人为中心直接带 Event-Ego / Emotion-Ego 标签的纯音频第一人称流（既往工作多以视觉为主）。
语音情感识别（SER）语音精选子集对情感特征明显的语句预标注、再由用户确认；日常中性语音保持稀疏。
音频描述（音频↔文本）综合推理精选子集模板化的「场景–事件–情绪」加自由备注 → 大模型扩写为 caption，用户校验。

直接 模式可直接产出该任务所需标签。   精选子集 面向显著、经预筛的子集，而非稠密覆盖。

6Open research directions开放研究方向

Beyond the established tasks above, the same data — longitudinal, first-person, self-reported emotion — opens a few directions with real-world demand. These are opportunities, not promises. 在上述成熟任务之外，同一份数据——纵向、第一人称、情绪由本人报告——还开启了若干具有现实需求的方向。以下是研究机会，而非承诺。

Personal mood forecasting

Use the preceding ambient context and emotion trajectory to anticipate near-term mood shifts.

Real-world need · digital wellbeing & early-warning support

Emotion-trigger discovery

Associate point events (a dropped glass, a difficult call) with the mood fluctuations they cause, to surface what small daily moments actually move someone's day.

Real-world need · behavioral health, reflective journaling

Passive wellbeing sensing

Low-burden, continuous affect and stress estimation from everyday ambient sound, validated against the wearer's own labels.

Real-world need · mobile health, eldercare check-ins

Personalized & on-device adaptation

Per-user, longitudinal data is a natural testbed for personalizing scene/event/emotion models and for privacy-preserving (on-device / federated) learning, since collection is consent-based and per-person.

Real-world need · private, on-device personalization

Audio life-logging & day narration

Reconstruct a structured, navigable summary of a day from ambient audio plus labels and notes — the "what happened today" view.

Real-world need · memory aids, personal informatics

Label-efficient audio learning

Treat the coarse, sparse, self-reported labels as weak supervision to pre-train audio representations and cut the cost of densely labeling real-world data.

Real-world need · scalable, affordable dataset construction

Selective uploads can bias a real-world dataset. Strong automated pre-labeling (VAD/SED and ASR recall) lowers the burden of keeping more data, which helps keep coverage representative — a practical concern worth designing for.

个人情绪预测

利用此前的环境上下文与情绪轨迹，预判近期的情绪变化。

现实需求 · 数字健康与情绪预警

情绪触发事件挖掘

将点状事件（打碎的杯子、一通棘手的电话）与其引起的情绪波动关联，揭示日常中哪些小事真正影响了一个人的一天。

现实需求 · 行为健康、反思式日记

被动健康感知

从日常环境音中进行低负担、连续的情绪与压力估计，并以佩戴者本人的标签作校验。

现实需求 · 移动健康、长者关怀

个性化与端侧适配

按用户、纵向的数据是个性化场景/事件/情绪模型与隐私保护（端侧 / 联邦）学习的天然试验场——因为采集本身基于知情同意、以个人为单位。

现实需求 · 隐私友好的端侧个性化

音频生活日志与日记叙事

由环境音加标签与备注，重建一份结构化、可浏览的「今天发生了什么」日记摘要。

现实需求 · 记忆辅助、个人信息学

标签高效的音频学习

把粗粒度、稀疏、自我报告的标签当作弱监督，用于预训练音频表征，降低真实世界数据稠密标注的成本。

现实需求 · 可扩展、低成本的数据集构建

选择性上传会给真实世界数据集带来偏差。强大的自动预标注（VAD/SED 与 ASR 回忆）能降低保留更多数据的负担，有助于让覆盖更具代表性——这是值得在设计中考量的现实问题。
