### LinkedIn / longer post

Sharing **Nuna-Edu-Toolkit** — an open, self-hostable toolkit from the AIoT Lab at The Chinese University of Hong Kong for building **first-person audio datasets**.

Audio captured by the *Nuna* wearable streams to a server **you** control, where it's sliced into 1-minute segments, pre-labeled with a **pluggable ASR model** (bring your own; Qwen3-ASR included), and annotated through a flexible, **event-driven block + point** schema. Participants stay in control of what they share.

✅ Self-hostable (one Docker Compose stack, SQLite)
✅ Pluggable, ASR-assisted recall
✅ Swappable, event-driven label schema
✅ Privacy-first · bilingual UI (EN / 中文)
✅ Source-available — free for non-commercial & research use

Try the live browser demo (no install) or self-host the full stack:
🔗 https://kevin-pal.github.io/nuna-edu-toolkit-release/
