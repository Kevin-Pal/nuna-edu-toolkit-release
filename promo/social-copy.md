## Post B — long (LinkedIn / 微信 / 小红书)

🚀 Excited to release **Nuna-Edu-Toolkit** — a **ThingX × AIoT Lab @ CUHK** collaboration that turns everyday sound into *first-person* audio datasets.

Capturing first-person audio at scale is hard. Nuna-Edu-Toolkit makes it a workflow you fully control:

🎙️ The **Nuna** wearable (by ThingX) streams ambient audio to **your own** server
✂️ Auto-sliced into 1-minute segments
🗣️ A **pluggable ASR** model (bring your own; Qwen3-ASR included) adds speech-to-text for fast recall
🧩 You label with a flexible, **event-driven block + point** schema
🔒 Privacy-first — participants choose what to share

✅ Self-hostable (one Docker Compose stack) · ✅ source-available, free for non-commercial & research use · ✅ bilingual UI (EN / 中文)

Try the live demo (no install) or self-host the full stack:
🔗 https://kevin-pal.github.io/nuna-edu-toolkit-release/

👋 At **MobiSys 2026**? Come find me to chat first-person audio datasets.
